⚠️ THE DANGER: Forgetting e.preventDefault() on forms/links causes instant page reloads, destroying your SPA state. One missing preventDefault = angry users losing their work.
✅ THE FIX:
e.preventDefault() - Stops default browser behavior
e.stopPropagation() - Stops event bubbling (different!)
Rule: ALWAYS call preventDefault() FIRST in event handler
Forms: Listen to 'submit' event, not 'click' on button
Links: Intercept 'click' event on <a> tags
